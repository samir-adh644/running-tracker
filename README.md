# FitTrack 🏃‍♂️

FitTrack is a lightweight, intuitive, and modern full-stack fitness tracking application designed to help users seamlessly monitor their physical activity, hydration, and overall health metrics. Built with a responsive layout, FitTrack offers an elegant dashboard to track daily goals and calculate key health indicators on the fly.

## ✨ Features

*   **🚶 Step Counter:** Real-time logging and tracking of daily steps walked to keep you moving toward your fitness goals.
*   **💧 Hydration Tracker:** Stay properly hydrated with an interactive water logging tool that tracks your daily intake against optimal standards.
*   **📏 Distance Travelled:** Automatically calculates the estimated mileage or kilometers traversed based on your activity metrics.
*   **🔢 Built-in BMI Calculator:** A built-in Body Mass Index (BMI) utility that instantly computes your score and provides health classification insights based on your height and weight inputs.
*   **📈 Progress Dashboard:** A clean, unified interface to visualize your daily metrics at a single glance.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

*   **Frontend:** React native Expo
*   **Backend and database:** Currently Asyncstorage
